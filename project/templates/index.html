Fruits are natural foods that come from plants. They are often sweet or sour and are packed with vitamins, minerals, and fiber, which help keep our bodies healthy. Fruits grow on trees, bushes, or vines and are usually eaten raw, although they can also be cooked or dried. Common examples of fruits include apples, bananas, oranges, berries, and mangoes. Eating a variety of fruits is good for overall health, as they provide essential nutrients and help keep our immune system strong.
